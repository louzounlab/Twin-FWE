This website was written by Yuli Tshuva at YOLO lab.


Welcome to the Pretwin Screen Project Biometry Calculator. This data is from a multinational, multicentred prospective study involving 527 twin pregnancies without major malformations and normal non-invasive prenatal screening. The study was presented at the 21st World Congress of the Fetal Medicine Foundation in Lisbon, Portugal in June 2024.


Please make sure to note down the following information: chorionicity, gestational age in weeks and days, and estimated fetal weight for both twins. After that, press submit. By entering multiple gestational ages and fetal weight estimates, you'll be able to create a growth curve for both fetuses.


It is imperative to verify all calculations before use. The estimated results cannot and should not replace clinical judgment.
